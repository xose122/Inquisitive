Features & Traits

Origin & General Feats
Fairy Trickster:
• Faerie Trod Trotter: When you take the Disengage action, Difficult Terrain doesn't cost extra movement for the rest of that turn.
• Flustering Strike (PB/LR): On a hit, target makes a WIS save (DC 16) or has Disadvantage on saves until end of your next turn.
Mage Slayer:
• Concentration Breaker: When you damage a creature concentrating, it has Disadvantage on the Concentration save.
• Guarded Mind (1/Short Rest): If you fail an INT/WIS/CHA save, you can succeed instead.
Rogue Class Features
Sneak Attack (5d6): 1/turn, on hit with Finesse or Ranged weapon. Need Advantage OR ally adjacent (no Disadv).
Thieves' Cant: Secret rogue language.
Weapon Mastery: Use mastery properties of 2 weapon types (changeable on Long Rest).
Cunning Action (Bonus Action): Dash, Disengage, or Hide.
Steady Aim (Bonus Action): Advantage on next attack this turn. Requires no movement; Speed becomes 0 until end of turn.
Cunning Strike (DC 16): Spend Sneak Attack dice for effects:
• Poison (1d6, requires Poisoner's Kit): CON save → Poisoned 1 min, save at end of each turn.
• Trip (1d6): DEX save → Prone (Large or smaller).
• Withdraw (1d6): Move up to ½ Speed without provoking OAs.
Uncanny Dodge (Reaction): Halve damage from one attack you can see (round down).
Evasion: On DEX save vs ½-damage effects: success = 0 dmg, failure = ½ dmg.
Reliable Talent: On any check using a skill or tool proficiency, treat d20 ≤ 9 as a 10.
Inquisitive
Heightened Senses (Lvl 3): Proficiency in Insight & Perception; PB doubled on those checks (effective Expertise — already shown).
Deductive Fighting Apprentice (Lvl 3): Bonus Action — Investigation check vs target's Deception. On success, mark target for 1 minute (or until you mark another):
• Deductive Offense: Use Sneak Attack vs that target without Advantage (still no Disadv).
• Deductive Defense: +INT (+3) to AC vs that target's attacks.
Steady Eye (Lvl 9): Advantage on Perception/Investigation if you moved ≤ ½ Speed this turn. Use the Search and Study actions as a Bonus Action.
Deductive Fighting Expert (Lvl 9):
• Deductive Offense: If you can already Sneak Attack target, gain Advantage on attack rolls vs it instead.
• Deductive Defense: When you take damage from target and use Uncanny Dodge, you can also make an Opportunity Attack against it as part of the same Reaction.

Ranged precision: Ranged weapon attacks ignore Half/Three-Quarters Cover. No Disadvantage at long range or vs creatures with Prone condition.
